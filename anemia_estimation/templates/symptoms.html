Symptoms of Anemia

Anemia manifests through various symptoms, which can vary in severity. Identifying these symptoms early can be crucial for effective management and treatment. Common symptoms include:

  • Persistent fatigue and weakness
  • Unusual paleness of the skin
  • Breathlessness or rapid heartbeat
  • Frequent dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Cold or numb extremities
  • Frequent headaches
  • Chest pain or angina
  • Brittle or spoon-shaped nails
  • Restless legs syndrome
